Privacy Policy

ATXO Labs · Effective date: May 25, 2026 · Last updated: May 25, 2026

Short version: Selam runs entirely on your Mac. Your conversations, your voice, and your API keys never leave your machine unless you explicitly send something (e.g. a WhatsApp message you asked Selam to send). ATXO Labs does not collect, store, or process your personal data on any server we operate.

1. Who we are

Selam is a product of ATXO Labs. When we say "we", "us", or "our", we mean ATXO Labs. You can reach us at legal@heyselam.com.

2. What data we collect — and what we do not

ATXO Labs does not operate servers that receive or store your conversations, voice recordings, camera captures, screen captures, or API keys. All of this data is processed and stored locally on your Mac, in the following locations:

  • API keys — stored in ~/.openclaw/secrets/ on your machine, readable only by your user account.
  • Conversation history — stored in ~/.openclaw/workspace/companion/session_history.json on your machine.
  • Session audit logs — stored in ~/Selam/logs/ on your machine. These logs record the actions Selam took on your behalf (e.g. "sent WhatsApp message", "ran agent task") for your own review.

We collect only what is strictly necessary to process your purchase:

  • Purchase data — your name and email address, collected by our payment processor Stripe at the time of purchase. We receive a copy of your email address to deliver your license key.

We do not operate analytics, crash reporting, telemetry, or any other form of usage tracking.

3. Third-party AI providers (your own accounts)

Selam is a Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) application. You supply API keys from third-party providers — Anthropic, ElevenLabs, Simli, and optionally DeepSeek or Google Gemini — and Selam uses those keys on your behalf from your Mac. Your API calls go directly from your machine to those providers' servers. ATXO Labs does not sit in the middle of those calls.

Each provider has its own privacy policy and data practices. You are entering into a direct relationship with them when you create an account. We strongly recommend reading their policies:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • ElevenLabs Privacy Policy
  • Simli Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Camera and microphone

Selam requests access to your Mac's microphone to hear your voice during sessions, and to your camera to respond to requests like "how do I look?" or "what's on my desk?". All captured frames are processed locally or sent directly to your configured AI provider (Anthropic) using your own API key. No audio or video is transmitted to ATXO Labs.

Camera and microphone access is granted by macOS and can be revoked at any time in System Settings → Privacy & Security.

5. Payment processing

Payments are processed by Stripe. Stripe collects and stores your payment card details, billing address, and related financial information. ATXO Labs does not receive or store your card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to all payment data: stripe.com/privacy.

6. License key delivery

After a successful purchase, your email address is used solely to deliver your license key. We retain your email address in order to process refund requests within the 14-day refund window and to provide support. We do not send marketing emails without your consent.

7. Cookies and web tracking

The Selam desktop application does not use cookies. Our marketing website (heyselam.com) may use minimal first-party analytics. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

8. Your rights

If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have rights under GDPR including access, correction, deletion, and objection to processing. To exercise these rights, email us at legal@heyselam.com. Because we hold only your email address, most requests can be fulfilled within 48 hours.

9. Children

Selam is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above and, where feasible, notify active users by email. Continued use of Selam after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy: legal@heyselam.com
