06 · Questions you're probably asking

The honest FAQ.

Do I need technical skills to set this up? +
You need to be comfortable creating accounts on a few services (Anthropic, ElevenLabs, Simli) and pasting API keys into a settings screen. We walk you through it in the first-run flow. If you can set up a Gmail account, you can set up Selam.
What's the real total cost? +
Selam is $199 one-time — that's it from us. On top of that you pay the AI providers directly: your chosen brain model (Haiku 4.5, Gemini 2.5 Flash, or DeepSeek V3), ElevenLabs for the voice, and Simli for the avatar. ElevenLabs Starter is $5/mo; Simli Hobby is $10/mo — both include generous monthly allowances. Brain costs depend heavily on which model you pick and how much you use it. Use the calculator above to dial in your exact number.
Can you see my conversations? +
No. Your API keys are stored locally in your macOS Keychain. Your conversations go directly from your Mac to Anthropic (for the brain), ElevenLabs (for the voice), and Simli (for the avatar). We don't have a server in the loop. We never see the content. We don't want to.
What can my agent actually do? +
Voice conversation with you. See your screen or webcam when you ask. Draft messages. Search your files. Open apps and URLs. Call other services through a task queue that you approve. Over time, more integrations (email, calendar, Slack, browser automation) ship as updates.
Is it safe to let it act on my behalf? +
Every action that touches the outside world — sending a message, making a purchase, deleting a file — goes through an approval gate. You see what it plans to do, and you click approve. Over time you can set policies ("always approve replies to my team", "never spend over $50 without asking"). Trust is built, not assumed.
If I stop using it, do I keep my agent's memory? +
Yes, completely. Your identity files (SOUL, IDENTITY, MEMORY, USER) are plain Markdown files in your home directory — not locked in any cloud. Walk away from Selam, keep the files. Come back later, pick up where you left off. Export them to another tool. Read them in your text editor. Edit them by hand. They're your agent's DNA, and you own it.
Windows? iPhone? Linux? +
macOS today (14+, Apple silicon recommended). Windows in Q3. Mobile is under design — it's a different technical stack, so it'll ship as a companion app after the desktop experience stabilizes. Linux is on the list for later; contact us if you want to be notified.
Why is it called Selam? +
Selam is Amharic for "peace" and it's also a common greeting in Ethiopia, where our founder is based. Our founder's agent is named Selam — she was the first. The platform is named after her because she proved the concept. Your agent will have their own name.
