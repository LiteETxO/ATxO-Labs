Terms

Plain‑English summary of how billing and bookings work for our pay‑per‑accepted‑demo service.

Definitions

  • ICP Fit: Target company matches agreed firmographics and tech signals; target persona matches agreed seniority/role.
  • Accepted Demo: (1) ICP Fit + (2) decision‑maker or strong influencer attends + (3) agenda matches product’s core value prop + (4) attended by both sides for ≥15 minutes within the scheduled window.

Billing Rules

  • Billable: Accepted Demos only.
  • Not Billable: No‑shows, reschedules beyond 7 days, non‑ICP, wrong persona, or calls under 15 minutes due to prospect cause.
  • Caps: Monthly spend cap applies (default $6,000). Billing pauses once the cap is reached unless extended in writing.

Process

  • Weekly review of lists, learning from replies, copy updates, and bookings.
  • Dispute window: 5 business days after the meeting; provide calendar + recording/notes for review.

Guarantee

14‑day money‑back if we book zero Accepted Demos in the first 14 days of live sending.

Data & Privacy

We access only the minimum necessary systems and data. You retain ownership of your data. See our Privacy Policy.
